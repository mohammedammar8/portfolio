A Software Engineer is a professional who designs, develops, tests, and maintains software applications and systems by applying principles from computer science and engineering. They work to create software that meets both user needs and business requirements, ensuring it is efficient, reliable, and scalable. Their role involves writing code, designing systems, and troubleshooting problems to ensure functionality. Software engineers also collaborate closely with other team members, such as developers, designers, and product managers, to deliver high-quality products.
